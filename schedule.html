The Iberian Private Equity Conference is a conference dedicated to Private Equity Management companies, funds, venture capitals, Limited Partners, wealth managers and lawyers that facilitates a unique platform for discussions, insights, and networking opportunities. It is a one-day concentrated event with exciting keynote speakers, thrilling panel discussions, and valuable networking opportunities for CEOs, partners, managing directors among other influential business people.
